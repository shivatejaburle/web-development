Sing a song of sing
Sing a song of 6, pence A pocket full of rye. 4 and 20 blackbirds, Big in a 5. When the pie was opened, The birds began to sing; and Wasn't that a dainty dish, To set before the king? The king was in his counting house, Counting out his money; The queen was in the parlor, Eating bread and honey. The maid was in the garden, Hanging out the cloth, When down came a blackbird And pecked off her nose.
