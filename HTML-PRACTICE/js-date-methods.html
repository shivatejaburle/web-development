JS date methods

  1. Date(): It will returns current date and time as a string
  2. getDate(): To get day (1 to 31)
  3. getMonth(): To get month (0 to 11)
  4. getFullYear(): To get year (yyyy)
  5. getDay(): To get weekday (0 to 6)
  6. getHours(): To get hours (0 to 23)
  7. getMinutes(): To get minutes (0 to 59)
  8. getSeconds(): To get seconds (0 to 59)
  9. getMilliseconds(): To get milliseconds (0 to 999)
